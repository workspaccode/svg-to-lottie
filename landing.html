About

We believe great tools make great products

Arc Studio was founded by a team of designers and engineers who were tired of bridging between disconnected tools. We built the platform we always wished existed — one that treats design as a system, not a series of screens.

Today, our remote team spans 14 countries and ships updates every single week. We're backed by First Round Capital and Sequoia, but we answer to our users — the thousands of product teams who trust us with their daily workflow.

We're intentionally small, intentionally focused, and we're in this for the long haul.
